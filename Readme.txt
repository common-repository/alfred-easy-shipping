== Description ==
 
By integrating Alfred Easy Shipping to your E-Commerce you will have:

* national and international couriers
* convenient delivery points for delivery to the end customer.
* You will provide various types of shipments at checkout, satisfying customers and optimizing your times and * those of your collaborators to manage orders, tracking and returns.

This plugin use api of https://postbox.alfrednet.eu and require subscription to link https://postbox.alfrednet.eu/alfred2/user/signup.php in order to use api and Alfred software interface.

== FAQ ==
= HOW DO I LAUNCH ALFRED EASY SHIPPING FOR WOOCOMMERCE? =
 
1. Download Plugin
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
3. Register for an Alfred account at https://postbox.alfrednet.eu/alfred2/user/signup.php
4. Receive your login details via email
5. Login to activate the plugin 
6. Configure local pickup pricing (see https://docs.woocommerce.com/document/local-pickup/) 
7. Your customers will then be able to select any Alfred pickup point at checkout.



Once the plugin is successfully installed, Alfred Easy Shipping will appear as delivery option on your checkout page. 
Remember to update your delivery policies and tell your customers about Alfred Easy Shipping!
